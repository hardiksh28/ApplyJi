Summary: ApplyJi helps you manage job applications. We collect only what's necessary to provide the service, never sell your data, and give you full control over your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated as you use ApplyJi:

Account Information

  • Name and email address when you sign up
  • Password (stored as a secure hash — never in plain text)
  • Profile details you choose to add (photo, bio, location)

Application Data

  • Job listings, company names, application statuses, notes, and dates you log
  • Documents you upload (resumes, cover letters)
  • Custom tags and categories you create

Usage Information

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps
  • Device information: operating system, screen resolution
  • Aggregated analytics on feature usage (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the Service: Create and manage your account, store your application data, and enable core features
  • Improve ApplyJi: Analyse usage patterns (in aggregate) to fix bugs and build better features
  • Communicate with You: Send account-related emails (verification, password reset, important updates)
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance: Meet our obligations under applicable law

We do not use your data to serve advertisements, and we never sell your personal information.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We share it only in these limited cases:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who help us operate (e.g., hosting, email delivery, analytics). They are contractually bound to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may transfer — we'll notify you beforehand.
  • With Your Consent: Any other sharing requires your explicit permission.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard safeguards including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest
  • Access controls — only authorized personnel can access production data
  • Regular security reviews and dependency updates

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage you to use a strong, unique password.

Retention: We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time, after which we remove your personal data within 30 days (except where retention is required by law).

5. Cookies & Tracking

ApplyJi uses cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential cookies: Keeping you logged in and saving preferences. These cannot be disabled without breaking the app.
  • Analytics cookies: Understanding how the app is used (e.g., Google Analytics or similar, in anonymized form).

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect core functionality.

6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information via your account settings
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Download your application data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-essential emails at any time

To exercise any right, email us at privacy@applyji.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

ApplyJi is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have done so, we will promptly delete the data. If you believe a child has provided us their information, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

ApplyJi may integrate with or link to third-party services (e.g., LinkedIn, job boards). This Privacy Policy does not apply to those services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before connecting them.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice in the app. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of ApplyJi after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

Questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy? Reach us at:

  • Email: privacy@applyji.com
  • Website: applyji.com

We take privacy concerns seriously and aim to respond within 5 business days.
